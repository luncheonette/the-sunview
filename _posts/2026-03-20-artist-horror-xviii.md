Please come to the luncheonette on Friday, March 20, for ARTIST-HORROR XVIII:
_The Grim Reaper_ (1961). Mysterious artist Henri Redin creates a painting of
the titular figure of death, and anyone who comes to own the work suffers a
violent demise. Will this artwork kill the famous author Beatrice Graves as
well? _Let us find out together on Friday_. Another dose of artistic melancholy
blurring the animate and inanimate, all woven into a killer painting. Starring
our beloved William Shatner and Mrs. Howell from _Gilligan's Island_. Free
pizza, free beer.  Hangouts at 7, film at 8.

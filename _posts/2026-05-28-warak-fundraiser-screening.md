stories while rolling a familiar favorite dish: stuffed grape leaves 🍃🍴

May 28th @ 7pm.

$15-$20 suggested donation that includes the screening + food, but no one will
be turned away for lack of funds. venmo @ hana-elias with “warak” in the caption
to RSVP & secure your spot. all proceeds will go toward small farms and
agriculture collectives in Palestine ahead of wheat harvest season 🌾

we’ll be serving a variety of home-cooked bites - including dolma, of course!!
(the event is BYOB).

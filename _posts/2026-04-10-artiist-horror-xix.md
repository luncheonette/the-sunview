Please come to the luncheonette on Friday, April 10, for ARTIST-HORROR XIX:
_Waxworks_ (1962). Pierre Jacquelin creates and exhibits an oeuvre of murderous
sculptures while collapsing the boundary between artist and artifact.
Animate/inanimate in the locus classicus of artist-horror: the wax museum. Free
pizza, free beer. Hangouts at 7, film at 8.

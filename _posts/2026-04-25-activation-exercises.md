**Activation Exercises: US launch concert for Stanisław Welbel’s new album, 
released digitally and on a limited cassette by the label Antenna Non Grata,
featuring a guest appearance by Marie-Andrée Pellerin.**

**Stanisław Welbel : Activation Exercises**<br>
[ANG MC38/2025]<br>
released March 12, 2026<br>
[Stanisław Welbel : Activation Exercises | Antenna Non Grata](https://antennanongrata.bandcamp.com/album/stanis-aw-welbel-activation-exercises)

The tracks were recorded between March 15 and 29, 2024 at the Ansible Institute
at Cafe Tissardmine, Moroccan Sahara. The Ansible Institute is a transitory
speculative fiction research laboratory, facilitated by Martina Raponi and [M]
Dudeck.<br>
Each track was created during so-called **activation exercises** with each of
the participants during Expedition 2 of the Ansible Institute: [M] Dudeck,
Martina Raponi, **Marie-Andrée Pellerin**, Brandon Gibson-DeGroote, Antoinette
Dyksman, Ralo Mayer, Niki Sperou, Stanisław Welbel, and the host at Cafe
Tissardmine: Karen Hadfield.

**Stanisław Welbel** (b. 1982, based in Warsaw) is a curator, art historian, and
a musician. He plays saxophones and electronic instruments. He regularly performs
with the Warsaw Improvisers Orchestra and used to play with the
Warszawsko-Lubelska Orkiestra Dęta. His previous solo album Poems was published
by Szara Reneta. [Poems | Stanisław Welbel | Szara
Reneta](https://szarareneta.bandcamp.com/album/poems)

**Marie-Andrée Pellerin** is a multidisciplinary artist from Montreal based in
Austria. Her artistic projects deal with territories, meteorology, science
fiction literature and speculative methods. She works with video, sound art,
performances, as well as textile art (tufting). Her art projects are carried in
site-specific contexts and residencies, through interdisciplinary collaborations
with authors, musicians and scientists from different fields.

<p>Hi Luncheonette:</p>
<p>For this Sunday's <strong>Club Night</strong> we will present an evening of readings, music, and tarot with friends in town visiting from <strong>UK</strong>, <strong>CANADA</strong>, and <strong>USA</strong></p>
<p>with–</p>
<p><strong>Ana Božičević &amp; Sophia Le Fraga (not_I),</strong><br />
<strong>Kari Cwynar,</strong><br />
<strong>David Morris,</strong><br />
<strong>Sarah Resnick,</strong><br />
<strong>Rosalie Schweiker.</strong></p>
<div class="columnbreak"></div>
<p><strong>COCKTAILS</strong> at 7<br />
<strong>SUPPER</strong> at 8<br />
<strong>READINGS</strong> at 8:30<br />
<strong>TAROT</strong> throughout</p>
<p><b>not_I </b>performs at galleries, universities, parties and weddings. For more information, contact Sophia Le Fraga and Ana Božičević.</p>
<p><b>Kari Cwynar </b>curates and writes and is Director of Kunstverein Toronto.</p>
<p><b>David Morris</b> is based in London. He is co-editor with Sylvère Lotringer of Schizo-Culture: The Event, The Book (Semiotexte/MIT, 2014).</p>
<p><b>Sarah Resnick</b> is a writer and managing editor of Triple Canopy.</p>
<p><b>Rosalie Schweiker</b> is a conceptual artist based in London. Together with Maria Guggenbichler she facilitated Funny Women Art, a holiday for female artists funded by the Munich arts council. Currently the two are working on the publication Clever Men's Art with Jeremy Deller, Harrell Fletcher, Peter Fischli &amp; David Weiss, Kenneth Goldsmith, Tino Sehgal, Franz West and others.</p>
<p><b>FUTpastURE</b> <b>press</b> publishes overdraft fees, laundry tickets, banana peels, and other expended/extended ephemera from their home base at the Sunview Luncheonette.  For this iteration they will be printing a poster/menu designed with Greg Mihalko (Partner &amp; Partners).</p>

On Saturday, December 13th we'll be hosting a benefit party for Poetry Clinic,
to raise funds for our poets. Please join us from 3pm-7pm at the Sunview
Luncheonette to gather for readings, music, limited-edition merch, zines,
cocktails and a pop-up kitchen.

Featuring: Reverend Billy and the Church of Stop Shopping • Somah Haaland •
Raphaëlle Khan • Jeremiah Lockwood • Alisha Mascarenhas • Grace Nissan • K
Prevallet • Trevor Smith • Kendra Sullivan • Sofia Wolfson

Tickets: [https://chuffed.org/project/160161-poetry-clinic-benefit](https://chuffed.org/project/160161-poetry-clinic-benefit)

This is a tiered-ticket fundraiser — all proceeds go directly toward paying
poets and community artists. A special dinner to happen afterwards as well. More
details to come soon. For now, save the date and invite your friends: Sat 13th
Dec, 3–7pm.

Address: 257 Nassau Avenue, Greenpoint, Brooklyn NYC 11222; Ground Floor

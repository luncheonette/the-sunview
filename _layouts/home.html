The Sunview Luncheonette is a storefront on pause, a member-based social club, a microvenue for art, poetics, regionalism, mutual aid, and commoning. It is resistant to traditional forms of commerce, commodification, and gentrification. It is above all, an "approach," and least of all, an "outcome." It is unknown. It is hopeful. It saves you a seat at the counter.
